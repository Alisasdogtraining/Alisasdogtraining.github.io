There are three main ways to reward a dog, food, play, and affection. Depending on your dogs personality you will have to try all methods and see which one your dog will work best for.

The key to sucsessful dog training is patience and time, you can train a dog anything with enough effort and persistance.

The key to a healthy and happy dog

Depending on your dog's weight and age you will need to walk them accordingly, for a small puppy you will need to walk them about 4 times a day, when they get older you will cut it to 3 and so on.

  • Small dog:1 finger
